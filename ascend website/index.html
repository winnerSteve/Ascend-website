Our Story

Built from
adversity,
built to last.

ASCEND was born when DECA — the global business education organization — suddenly banned all international chapters, leaving hundreds of schools without a home. Rather than accept that loss, we built something better.

The Association of Student Commerce, Entrepreneurship, and New Developing Leaders is a fully independent international organization designed specifically for international schools — providing everything DECA offered and more, on a global stage.

From business competitions and international conferences to Spirit Store operations and leadership development, ASCEND gives students the arena to compete, grow, and rise to the top.

Program 02 — International Conferences

Where the
world meets.

Once a year, ASCEND brings together the brightest student minds from member schools across the globe for three days of competition, learning, and connection that no classroom can replicate.

Conference Experience

Three days.
Unforgettable.

The ASCEND International Conference is the centrepiece of the organization's calendar — a fully immersive event that combines the intensity of competition finals with the energy of a global gathering of ambitious students.

01
Competition Finals

All six competition categories hold their International Finals live at the conference. Students who have advanced through the Preliminary and Regional rounds compete in front of industry judges and a live audience of their peers.

02
Keynote Speakers

Executives and leaders from ASCEND's partner organizations take the main stage to share insights on industry trends, career paths, and the skills that define success in the modern business world. Candid, honest, and directly relevant.

03
Industry Workshops

Hands-on sessions led by partner organization professionals covering strategy, marketing, finance, sustainability, and more. Students leave with practical skills, real frameworks, and direct exposure to how the world's best organizations operate.

04
Global Networking

Structured and unstructured networking opportunities connect students across schools, countries, and cultures. These are relationships built on shared ambition — the kind that last long beyond ASCEND and into professional life.

Conference Agenda

Three days,
designed with intent.

Every minute of the ASCEND conference is intentional. The agenda balances high-pressure competition with genuine learning and real human connection — because the best leaders know when to perform and when to listen.

Day One — Arrival & Opening
09:00
Delegate Registration

Check-in, welcome packs, and school introductions. Delegates connect with peers from other member schools.

14:00
Opening Ceremony

ASCEND leadership addresses delegates. Partner executives share opening remarks. The competition season officially begins.

16:00
Industry Workshop — Session I

First partner-led workshop. All delegates participate in groups rotating across topics.

19:30
Welcome Dinner & Networking

Formal dinner with structured networking. Delegates seated across school lines to encourage cross-school connection.

Day Two — Competition & Learning
08:30
Competition Finals — Morning

Case Study Analysis and Entrepreneurship Pitch finals. Industry judges. Live audience.

12:30
Keynote Speaker — Partner Executive

Main stage address from a senior leader at one of ASCEND's founding partner organizations.

14:00
Competition Finals — Afternoon

Marketing Strategy, Finance & Investment, and Sustainability Challenge finals.

17:30
Industry Workshop — Session II

Second partner-led workshop with different facilitators and topic areas.

Day Three — Finals & Awards
09:00
International Trade Finals

Final competition category. Live negotiation simulations judged by international business professionals.

12:00
Closing Keynote & Reflection

A final address from ASCEND leadership. Delegates reflect on the three-day experience together.

18:00
Awards Ceremony & Gala

Black-tie awards ceremony. Category winners announced. ASCEND's most memorable evening of the year.
